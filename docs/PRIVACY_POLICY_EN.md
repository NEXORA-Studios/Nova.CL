# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 29, 2025**

Welcome to Nova.CL! We take your privacy and personal information protection very seriously. This Privacy Policy aims to inform you about how we collect, use, store, and protect the personal information you provide or generate when using the Nova.CL application (hereinafter referred to as "the Application").

## 1. Information Collection

### 1.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Application, we may automatically collect the following information:

- **Device Information**: Including device model, operating system version, device identifiers, CPU information, memory information, etc.
- **Application Usage Information**: Including application startup time, usage duration, feature usage, error logs, etc.
- **Network Information**: Including IP address, network type, connection status, etc.

### 1.2 Information You Voluntarily Provide

When using specific features of the Application, you may need to voluntarily provide the following information:

- **Account Information**: Such as Minecraft account information (only used for game-related features)
- **Configuration Information**: Such as application settings, personalized configurations, etc.
- **Feedback Information**: Such as questions, suggestions, etc. you submit through the feedback feature

## 2. Information Usage

The information we collect is mainly used for the following purposes:

- Provide, maintain, and improve the features and services of the Application
- Diagnose and resolve issues during application use
- Optimize application performance and user experience
- Send you important update notifications and security alerts
- Develop new features and services

## 3. Information Storage and Protection

### 3.1 Information Storage

- We will store the collected information on secure servers or your local device
- Locally stored information mainly includes application configuration, cache data, etc.
- Server-stored information mainly includes account-related data, error logs, etc.

### 3.2 Information Protection

We have adopted multiple security measures to protect your personal information:

- Data Encryption: Encrypting sensitive data for storage and transmission
- Access Control: Strictly limiting access rights to personal information
- Regular Security Audits: Regularly checking and updating security measures
- Data Backup: Regularly backing up data to prevent data loss

## 4. Information Sharing

We will not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party unless:

- We obtain your explicit consent
- Required by law or in response to legal proceedings
- To protect our rights, property, or safety, or the rights, property, or safety of others
- Shared with our affiliated companies or service providers (only for service purposes, and they are required to comply with privacy protection obligations)

## 5. Your Rights

Under applicable law, you may have the following rights:

- Access, correct, or delete your personal information
- Withdraw consent for information collection and use
- Request us to stop processing your personal information
- Request us to transfer your personal information to other service providers

## 6. Children's Privacy

The Application is not designed or developed for children under the age of 13. We do not intentionally collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a child's guardian and discover that we have collected a child's personal information, please contact us, and we will immediately take measures to delete the relevant information.

## 7. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be published within the Application or through our official channels, with the "Last Updated" date updated. We recommend that you regularly review this Privacy Policy to understand changes to our privacy protection practices.
